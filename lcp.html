Overview

Campaigns
that convert

Lane Clark & Peacock had an established creative team when I joined as Senior Designer. My role was to push the quality ceiling, bringing a more strategic and elevated approach to how one of the UK's leading actuarial consultancies presented itself to clients and new business prospects. Working closely with Senior Consultants and Partners, I produced campaign materials that directly supported client acquisition at the highest level, while mentoring junior designers to raise the overall standard across the team.

A significant part of my contribution was modernising how the firm delivered thought leadership. I introduced Foleon as the platform for interactive, web-ready publications, replacing static PDFs with engaging digital experiences across reports, microsites, and event collateral. The measurable uplift in audience engagement on key publications proved the commercial case for design investment at this level.

Motion & Video

Content in motion

As the only person at Lane Clark & Peacock with video editing and motion graphics capability, I became the firm's sole resource for every animated and video asset it produced. LCP leaned heavily into motion as a communication tool, and every piece came through me: animated data visualisations for pension scheme reports, event intro reels, social content, and client-facing campaign films. I developed a fast, repeatable production approach that maintained quality at volume, adapting tone and style across authoritative thought leadership and high-energy event content alike. In a firm where motion was not the norm, I made it central.
