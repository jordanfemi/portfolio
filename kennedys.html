Overview

Global design
at scale

Joining Kennedys Law LLP as Senior Brand & Digital Designer, I was brought in to build a creative function that could operate at the scale of a 2,900-person global law firm. What followed was two years of systemic design leadership: brand standards enforced across 45 offices in 18 countries, an in-house motion and video capability built from scratch, and two major integrated campaigns produced entirely without external studios.

This page documents the full output — from the brand infrastructure and DAM system that underpins everything, to the Global Strategic Plan campaign (seven films, a stakeholder presentation deck, and a full suite of digital assets) and the Kennedys Foresight 2026 campaign (regional LinkedIn carousels across UK, US and APAC, email communications, social video, and a teaser film). Every asset on this page was conceived, designed, and produced in-house.

Impact

A creative
function that
lasts.

The output documented on this page tells one story: a creative function that went from scattered and reactive to structured, strategic, and genuinely ambitious. Two major integrated campaigns. Fourteen campaign videos produced in-house. Regional content adapted across three global markets. A stakeholder presentation that aligned the firm's senior leadership on a single creative direction. Email, social, print, and digital collateral all produced without a single external studio brief.

That scale of output only happens when the infrastructure is right — when the brand guidelines, the DAM system, the production pipelines, and the team culture are all pulling in the same direction. Building that infrastructure is what this role has been. The campaigns are the proof of what it makes possible.
